**1.Brief Summary of the capstone project**

Explore Incredible India is a multi-page cultural heritage website built entirely using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It is designed to showcase the richness, diversity, and greatness of India — covering its iconic destinations, traditional art forms, cultural heritage, festivals, dance forms, and historical contributions to the world.

The website works like a real application with a login and logout system, where users can register an account, sign in, or continue as a guest. Once logged in, they can explore five content-rich pages — Home, Destinations, Art \& Crafts, Heritage \& Tradition, and Gallery — each filled with cards, images, animations, and informative descriptions about India.

The project was built as a Capstone Project to demonstrate core web development skills including UI design, responsive layout, CSS animations, and JavaScript form validation — all without any backend framework or external database.



**2. Features**

**Feature 1 — Login \& Registration System**



Users can Register with name, username, email, and password

Users can Sign In with username and password

Guest access available to skip login

Session is saved using localStorage so users stay logged in on refresh

Logout button clears the session and returns to login screen

Passwords are encoded before storing



**Feature 2 — Five-Page Navigation**



Home — Overview of India's greatness, ancient civilisation, world contributions, statistics, and contact form

Destinations — 20+ famous places and hidden gems across India

Art \& Crafts — 12 traditional paintings, 8 handicrafts, and 4 textile traditions

Heritage \& Tradition — Festivals, classical dance forms, traditional dress, and music heritage

Gallery — Animated circle gallery, image grid, stories of India, and Did You Know facts



**Feature 3 — Responsive Design**



Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile screens

CSS Grid with auto-fill and minmax() makes cards automatically reflow

Images use object-fit: cover so they never stretch

Navbar adapts on smaller screens



**Feature 4 — CSS Animations**



Header slides down on page load

Cards lift up and image zooms on hover

Banner text fades in with scale effect

Scroll reveal — cards fade up as you scroll down the page

Circle gallery — images float with orbital animations

Navbar links have an underline slide-in effect on hover

Pulse animation on the center map image in the gallery



**Feature 5 — Form Validation**



Contact form on the Home page validates:

All three fields (Name, Email, Message) must be filled

Email must contain @ and follow correct format using regex

Error message appears in red below the button without page reload

Success alert appears when all fields are valid



**Feature 6 — Content Depth**



20+ tourist destinations including Hidden Gems

12 traditional painting styles across India

8 classical and folk dance forms

8 festival cards with descriptions

8 traditional dress styles from different states

3 music heritage traditions

8 ancient contributions India gave to the world

12 Did You Know facts in the Gallery

6 India by the Numbers statistics

Mark Twain quote section on the Home page
