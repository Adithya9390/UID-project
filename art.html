India is famous for its rich tradition of art and crafts. Each region has its own unique style, materials, and techniques. These crafts reflect India's culture, history, and creativity passed down through generations.

Traditional Paintings

Madhubani Art

Famous folk art from Bihar known for bright colors and patterns.

Warli Painting

Tribal art from Maharashtra using simple geometric shapes.

Tanjore Painting

Rich paintings from Tamil Nadu with gold decoration.

Handicrafts

Pottery

Clay art used for making pots and decorative items.

Wood Carving

Detailed carvings on wood used in furniture and decor.

Metal Crafts

Beautiful items made using brass, copper, and silver.

Textiles & Embroidery

Kantha Work

Traditional embroidery from West Bengal.

Bandhani

Tie-dye textile art from Gujarat and Rajasthan.

Kashmiri Shawls

Famous for soft wool and intricate designs.

Did You Know?

India has hundreds of unique art forms across different states.

Many crafts are handmade and eco-friendly.

These traditions are passed down for generations.
